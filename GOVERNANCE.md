# Governance

WTV is maintained as an open draft standard.

## Roles

- Maintainers: manage releases, review PRs, and keep the process moving.
- Reviewers: provide technical review for specs, security, wallets, and interoperability.
- Contributors: propose changes through issues, discussions, and PRs.

## Decision Process

- Editorial fixes may be merged after maintainer review.
- Normative changes require public discussion before merge.
- Breaking changes are allowed before `WTV-v1` is frozen.
- After `WTV-v1` is frozen, breaking changes require a new version label.

## Working Group

A lightweight working group may be formed when multiple independent organizations actively participate.

Initial focus areas:

- wallet implementation feedback;
- transaction and chain validation;
- trust metadata and revocation;
- test vectors and interoperability.

## Maintainer Policy

Maintainer access should be granted gradually based on sustained public contribution and review quality.
